About Us

Okwami Okwethu Foundation is a South African based social and environmental justice NPC that focuses on issues such as, but not limited, to taking action against the impact of the Covid-19 pandemic on affected individuals and communities, climate change literacy, the creation of more eco-friendly environments in poor communities and provide skills revolution for socio-economic redress.

Our Vission

To create a more environmentally-aware and eco-friendly society that honors all roles played in the ecosystem, that implements sustainable measures in all aspects of life and always has future generations in mind whilst bringing relief to current social problems.

Our Mission

Okwami Okwethu Foundation seeks to better the understanding of everyday people on environmental issues and help them take a stand against climate change in their personal capacities. Also, the foundation helps specifically Covid-19 pandemic distressed individuals.
